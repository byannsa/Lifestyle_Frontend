Iphone 16 Pro Max Review

There is a new Apple chip of course, one that the maker advertises as the next big thing. The A18 Pro has a faster six-core processor and more powerful Apple GPU with expanded and improved hardware support for features like raytracing and more. The memory bandwidth has been increased for faster AI tasks that will be needed for Apple Intelligence.

The cameras have always been a strong selling point for the iPhones and the triple-camera setup on the 16 Pro Max has become even better. While the 48MP primary and the 12MP 5x telephoto remain mostly the same (Fusion camera and next-gen sensor shift stabilization aside), there is a new 48MP ultrawide-camera for even more impressive panoramic photos. Now the Pro model supports up to 4K120fps video capturing, spatial clips, there are studio-quality microphones, audio mixing options for videos, and more.
