Iphone 15 Pro Max Review

The iPhone 15 Pro Max introduces Apple's A17 Pro chip, offering a faster six-core CPU and enhanced GPU with improved ray tracing capabilities. Its performance boost enhances tasks like AI processing, graphics, and gaming.

The camera system remains a key feature with a 48MP primary and 12MP telephoto lens. The ultrawide camera is also 48MP, delivering richer panoramic photos. Video recording has been upgraded to support 4K at 60fps, spatial video, and advanced audio features such as studio-quality microphones.
