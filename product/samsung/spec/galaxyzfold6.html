Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 Review

The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 introduces improved durability, with a lighter hinge and a more robust folding mechanism. Its 7.6-inch main display provides a seamless multitasking experience, while the external display offers a smoother 120Hz refresh rate.

Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, the performance is top-tier for productivity and gaming. The 50MP triple camera setup excels at capturing vivid photos with optical zoom and wide-angle shots, along with enhanced video recording capabilities, including 8K at 30fps.
